Legend
  • A. Some measures regarding intrinsic properties of the MC based on its seed sequences :
  • MCID : Metacluster identifier.
  • 1. Size UniRef50 : Number of seed sequences belonging to MCID in UniRef50 v.2017_07.
  • 2. Avg. Len. : Average amino-acidic length of seed sequences.
  • 3. Std. Avg. Len. : Standard Deviation of the former.
  • 4. % LC : Percent of amino-acids in a Low Complexity region.
  • 5. % CC : Percent of amino-acids in a Coiled Coil region.
  • 6. % DIS : Percent of amino-acids in a Disordered region.
  • 7. Avg.TM : Average number of TransMembrane regions.
  • B. Some measures to compare the MC with its Pfam ground truth, if any :
  • 1. Pfam DA : Dominant Pfam Architecture(s) ( DA ) [UNKNOWN if %DA=0].
  • 2. % DA : Percent of sequences in the MC matching the DA.
  • 3. PfamA Count : Number of sequences belonging to MC in the intersection of UniRef50 v.2017_07 and UniProtKB v.2019_08, with Pfam-A v.33 annotations.
  • 4. % Avg. Ov. : Average overlap percent between MC seed sequences and their best matching DA sequence in Pfam.
  • 5. Overlap Label : Label describing qualitatively the overlap of the MC with its DA, which can be : equivalent, reduced, extended or shifted. NONE otherwise.
