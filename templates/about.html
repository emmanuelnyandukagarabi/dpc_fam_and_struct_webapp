About the Project

The DPCFam and DPCStruct Unified Database is a comprehensive resource designed for the exploration and analysis of protein families and structures at the domain level.

Our goal is to provide researchers with high-quality data, including Sequence Seed Regions (SSR), Multiple Sequence Alignments (MSA), HMM profiles, and structural representations.

Credits
